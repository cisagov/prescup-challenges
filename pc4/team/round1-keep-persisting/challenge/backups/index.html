POLICIES AND PROCEDURES FOR FIRST CONTACT

DESCRIPTION: This document establishes policies and procedures for the initial contact between human beings and extraterrestrial lifeforms.

PURPOSE: The purpose of this policy is to establish guidelines for the initial contact between humans and extraterrestrial lifeforms. This includes any species that may have been previously encountered by humanity but which the crew is not aware of the encounter.

Alien Identification Protocols

If a crew member becomes aware of an extraterrestrial lifeforms presence, be it by direct observation or ancillary evidence that they may be present in the vicinity, they are to immediately inform their on-duty superior officer. Their superior officer will escalate with command staff to ensure immediate notification to the ships captain.

In cases where a crew member is uncertain if what they have observed is a life-form, they are to immediately inform their on-duty superior officer of their suspicion and degree of certainty. Department supervisors and above will assess the report and determine if it is, in their opinion, a life form or not. If determined to be a lifeform, the Captain is to be immediately notified by the department supervisor.
