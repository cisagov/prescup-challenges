🦖 History is broken. Agent404 is trapped. Fix the timeline.

While on a mission in prehistoric times, your partner was captured by a roaming band of cavemen! However, something is wrong with their time watch and the time device at `time_device.pre.pccc`, and they cannot escape. Your Agent ID for this mission is `Agent127`, and your partner is `Agent404`. You can download a CA cert for the sites from `https://watch_agent127.pre.pccc/ca.crt`. 

## Getting Started
Connect to your time watch at `https://watch_agent127.pre.pccc` and try to rescue your partner! The token is provided when you successfully help Agent404 `/escape`.
