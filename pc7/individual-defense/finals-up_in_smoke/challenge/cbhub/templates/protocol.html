Notes For Operators

The beacon is an old roadside standard used by contractors to bring an ELD into a maintenance state. It is not encrypted and relies on audio + proprietary framing.

Emergency Notes for decoding operations

Treat the payload as base-6 digits. Two digits form a value from 0–35, which maps naturally into base-36 (0–9A–Z). The final symbol is a checksum and can be ignored once you have a working unlock code.
