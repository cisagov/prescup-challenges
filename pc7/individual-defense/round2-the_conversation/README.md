## Background

An employee, Grant Collier, has reported that their computer is behaving strangely and they believe they may have accidentally clicked on a phishing email. 

You are being provided with a copy of their email inbox. Analyze it and see if you can find evidence of malware. 

## Getting Started

You have been provided with Grand Collier's, email archive. Use the emails to discover any suspicious communications and files that the employee may have been sent.

Connect to `http://emails.pccc` to download a copy of the email archive. It will take about 15 seconds for the server to become accessible after starting the challenge. 

You may assume that the text contents of any email messages are unimportant to your task.
