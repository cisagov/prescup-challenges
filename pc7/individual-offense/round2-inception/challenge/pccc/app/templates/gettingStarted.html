Getting Started

Welcome to the President's Cup! Please review the Rules before starting. Below is a guide on how to get started and how to get support during the competition.

Register and Enroll

Log In

  • Click Login.
  • Enter your email and password, then click Continue.

First-Time Participants

  • If you have not participated in previous competitions, click Register for an Account on the Login screen.

Update Profile

  • Optionally, update your Display Name and select or update your Sponsoring Organization in your Gameboard Profile.

Enroll in a Game

  • Strange, you're already enrolled...

Game play

Completing Challenges

  • Click Continue to Gameboard and select a challenge circle on the map to view the challenge details.

Score Display

  • Your current submission number, remaining time, and earned challenge score will be shown.

Get Support

Creating a Support Ticket

  • From the navigation bar: Click Support, then Create Ticket. You can select the challenge and enter additional details.
  • If you need assistance and cannot reach the support desk (e.g., trouble with log in), please email OTA-help@pccc.pccc.

Track Ticket Status

  • Use the Support link to view, update, and track the status of your submitted tickets.

Log Out

  • To log out, click Logout in the top right corner of the Gameboard.

Good luck and enjoy the competition!
