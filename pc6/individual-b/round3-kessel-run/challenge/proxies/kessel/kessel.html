Kessel, The Legendary Cipher Mining Planet

With the dangers of the Hackadese Maelstrom safely behind you, you can see Kessel straight ahead. The planet surface is a sickly orange, tainted by the cryptomining of the illicit substance known on the Deep Web as "Cypher".

Unfortunately, your luck is only getting worse. The Empire has formed a massive Firewall around the planet consisting of thousands of AI fighters and 5 of the Empire's largest war ships, no-Execute-class Destroyers. Even a near miss from one of their many cannons could render the Millennium Packet as read-only memory...

Ignoring the odds calculated by your CSPRNG, you switch off all power and begin drifting towards Kessel...
